## Purpose of This Form

This form records your informed consent for Invegent to create a personalised AI avatar on your behalf using HeyGen or equivalent avatar generation technology. The avatar will be used to produce video content as part of your ICE content service subscription.

Please read this form carefully before signing. If you have any questions, contact hello@invegent.com before proceeding.

---

## Section 1 — What an AI Avatar Is

An AI avatar is a digital representation of a real person, created by training an AI model on video footage of that person. The resulting avatar can be animated to speak scripted text in a realistic manner, producing video content that resembles the original person speaking to camera.

Invegent uses HeyGen (heygen.com) to create and render avatar videos. Your avatar data is processed and stored on HeyGen's platform in accordance with their privacy policy (heygen.com/privacy-policy).

---

## Section 2 — What You Are Consenting To

By signing this form, you authorise Invegent to:

1. **Create a digital avatar** of the person named below using video footage you provide
2. **Store your avatar** on HeyGen's platform for the duration of your ICE subscription
3. **Generate video content** using your avatar based on scripts approved through the ICE content pipeline
4. **Publish avatar videos** to the social media platforms specified in your ICE service agreement
5. **Clone your voice** (if opted in below) to match the avatar's lip movements using ElevenLabs or equivalent voice synthesis technology

---

## Section 3 — What You Are NOT Consenting To

Invegent will NOT:

- Use your avatar to create content that has not been approved through the ICE pipeline
- Use your avatar to make statements you have not authorised
- Share your avatar data with any third party other than HeyGen (for avatar rendering) and ElevenLabs (for voice synthesis, if opted in)
- Use your avatar after this consent is withdrawn or your ICE subscription ends
- Create content that misrepresents your professional credentials, opinions, or identity

---

## Section 4 — Content Approval

All video scripts generated by ICE will go through the standard approval workflow before being rendered using your avatar. You retain full control over what content is produced:

- Scripts are reviewed and approved by you (or your designated representative) before rendering
- You may reject any script without needing to provide a reason
- Rendered videos are published only after your approval, or under auto-approval rules you have explicitly configured

- You remain personally responsible for all content published using your avatar and your professional registration
- ICE does not provide legal or compliance advice — review all content against your own professional obligations

---

## Section 6 — Data Handling and Retention

**Avatar training footage:** Video footage you provide for avatar creation will be uploaded to HeyGen and is not stored by Invegent beyond the upload process.

**Avatar ID:** Invegent stores only the HeyGen Avatar ID (a reference string) in the ICE database — not the underlying avatar model or footage.

**Voice clone:** If voice cloning is enabled, an ElevenLabs Voice ID is stored. The underlying voice model is held by ElevenLabs in accordance with their privacy policy.

**Rendered videos:** Video files rendered using your avatar are stored in Invegent's Supabase storage (Sydney, Australia) for the duration of your subscription and deleted within 30 days of subscription termination.

**Deletion:** You may request deletion of your avatar from HeyGen and your voice clone from ElevenLabs at any time by contacting hello@invegent.com. Deletion will be completed within 14 days.

---

## Section 7 — Withdrawal of Consent

You may withdraw this consent at any time by notifying Invegent in writing at hello@invegent.com. On withdrawal:

- Avatar video production will cease immediately
- Your avatar will be deleted from HeyGen within 14 days
- Your voice clone will be deleted from ElevenLabs within 14 days (if applicable)
- Previously published videos will not be automatically removed — you must request removal separately through each platform
